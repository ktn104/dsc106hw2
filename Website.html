Data exploration to discover the affect of Burger King's Veggie Burgers


This chart displays the stability of sales throughout the month of January 2016.

Conclusion

The data shows an initial major drop in sales when Burger King first released the burger but sales naturally increased the months following. The sales of 2018 was still the highest despite the release of the veggie burger in the later half of the year. Even though there was an initial drop in sales due to the release of the veggie burger it does not seem to have a major lasting effect.

Pitfalls

Our team wasn't able to analyze the long term effects of the Veggie Burger. We do not know if the sales decreased the following year due to the Veggie Burger. We also do not know if the decrease in sales following the release of the Veggie Burger was the sole cause of the decrease in sales. There could have been many other factors to the decrease in sales. We also do not know why sales increased so much from the year 2016. The increase in sales these past two years might have been out of the ordinary and that the sales might have just been returning to normal after a while. We also could not see a specific trend in the regional data. As a result our major pitfalls is unable to isolate the effect of the Veggie Burger and also see the long term effect of it due.




Success

The data visualization showed that there could be a correlation between the release of the veggie burger and a decrease in overall sales. We do not know if there were other factors involved but the data points in that direction. The data visualization also showed that there might just be a trend in overall decrease in sales over the last few months of the year. The eventually rebounded after a few months of the release of the Veggie Burger so it seems like it did not affect sales long term and only decrease sales momentary. The charts also showed an evenly distributed amount in sales between all the regions and a similar trend in all of them. As a result the charts potential showed an initial drop in sales due to the Veggie Burger but it does not seem like the release of the Veggie Burger had a long term effect.
